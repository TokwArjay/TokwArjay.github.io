About TechMaid Games

Welcome to TechMaid Games, it is a branch-off company from an already existing company, TechMaid Bistro. Whatever TechMaid Games may or may not do is completely of its own volition and no decision were made by the TechMaid Bistro Owners, simply put, TechMaid Games is on its own.

TechMaid Games is a brand-new name aiming to enter the game industry as indie developer. TechMaid Games aims to combine learning and gaming by implementing game elements as a way of learning things. It aims to make learning enjoyable and more bearable. TechMaid Games aims to produce a free game that would benefit Students the most, games that won't need much time but still provides content to play and enjoy

TechMaid Games is ran by a single person, goes by the nickname "Kuukay", he is a College Student and currently taking the course of Computer Engineering Technology. He started this "company" out of boredom during his Vacation, June 2025. He spent his free time creating games in an unorthodox way and rather inefficient and stressful way, he enjoys it either way. He started this shenanigans hoping to struck gold and make some pocket money if ever.
